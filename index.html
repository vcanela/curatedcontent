Medicine & Science Research Hub

A curated collection of sources for your Year 9 research project. Explore each topic area and use the resources below to support your investigation.

How to Use This Hub

  1. Choose a topic from the cards below or the navigation bar above.
  2. Each topic page lists available sources. Read at least two sources per topic.
  3. Take notes on the key information each source provides.
  4. Record which sources you use in your citation log, and explain why you chose them.
  5. Compare what different sources say about the same topic.

What to Look For

  • Who wrote this? What are their credentials?

  • Does it cite evidence?
  • Does the language seem balanced or emotional?
  • Who published it, and why?
