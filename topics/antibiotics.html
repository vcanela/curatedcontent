Antibiotics & Resistance

From Fleming's accidental discovery to the global threat of antibiotic resistance — explore how these drugs work and why their future is uncertain.

Antibiotics: Mechanisms, History, and the Resistance Crisis

A comprehensive scientific overview covering how antibiotics disrupt bacterial cell processes, the history of their development, and the mechanisms driving antimicrobial resistance. Includes data from WHO and peer-reviewed studies.

Source 2

The Superbug Crisis: Are We Running Out of Antibiotics?

A magazine-style feature on antibiotic resistance, profiling real cases of drug-resistant infections and asking whether medicine is prepared for a "post-antibiotic era." Accessible and urgent in tone.

Source 3

Stop Reaching for the Pill Bottle: My Family's Journey Off Antibiotics

A personal blog post from a father who stopped using antibiotics for his children after reading about resistance. Questions whether modern medicine is "overdoing it" and advocates for natural immune support.
