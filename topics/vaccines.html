Topic Area

Vaccines

Explore the history of vaccination, the science behind how vaccines work, and how different sources frame the debate around vaccine use.

Topic Overview

Vaccines are one of the most significant public health achievements of the modern era. From Edward Jenner's smallpox experiments in 1796 to the rapid development of COVID-19 vaccines, vaccination has transformed human health outcomes worldwide.

This topic covers the biological mechanisms of immunity, the historical development of key vaccines, and the ongoing public conversation about vaccine safety and uptake.
